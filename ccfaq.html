How do my coupons and promotions get published in the app?

Your welcome email sent to you following your registration will contain a link to upload your marketing materials.

What happens when I want to change my promotions?

The same process as initial setup allows you to change your promotions as often as you see fit. Just remember you are only allotted 3 promotions at any given point in time.

What are the payment options?

We use Stripe for subscription payments which allows you many different options for payment. If you don't see an option that fits your needs please email us at Contact@RookeryUS.com

Are the analytics associated with my promotions available to me?

Currently you will receive a monthly analytics report detailing your interactions with our community.
