WHAT IS THE PURPOSE OF COMMUNITY CHEST?

Community Chest offers a new avenue for small business marketing. Local businesses advertising for other local businesses. Dominos doesn't refuse to advertise in the same flyer as Pizza Hut.

WHAT IS THE GOAL FOR BUSINESSES THAT JOIN?

If local businesses promote eachother they can build a customer base to rival the large corporations. Togetherness is the goal of Community Chest!
